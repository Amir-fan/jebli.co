Frequently Asked Questions

How long does delivery take?

Typical delivery time is 7 to 10 days from order confirmation. We'll keep you updated throughout the process.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash payments at our office after order confirmation. This ensures security for both parties.

Can I track my order?

Yes! Use our order tracking system to monitor your package's journey from Turkey to Syria.

What if my item is out of stock?

We'll contact you immediately if an item becomes unavailable and offer alternatives or a full refund.

Do you offer customer support?

Absolutely! Our team is available 24/7 via WhatsApp and email to assist with any questions.

What are your service fees?

We charge a 15% service fee plus $6 per kg for shipping. All fees are transparent and calculated upfront.
